Data Viz Kids

Bring data literacy to middle schools.

A UW Informatics Capstone Project

Our project works to instill data literacy curriculum in middle school classrooms to help students better question, understand and draw meaningful conclusions from data.

Why our curriculum is needed

Modern methodologies

Current education systems stick to traditional methodologies that fail to provide students with the comprehensive skillset to interact effectively with information in a data driven world.

Relevance in industry

The ability to read, work with, analyze, and argue with data crucial in various industries of today's workforce.

Forgiving learning curve

We aim to linearize the learning curve of data literacy across secondary education.

Interdisciplinary

Our curriculum is adaptable to cater to teachers that are focused on niche topic areas to further promote a diverse and interdiciplinary education amongst students.
